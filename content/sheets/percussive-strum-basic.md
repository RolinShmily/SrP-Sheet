## Playing Notes

The muted hits should sound like a soft snare, not a slap. Rest the fretting fingers lightly across the strings on the marked beats and keep the right hand moving through the whole bar.

## Practice Prompt

Loop two bars and make the muted strokes equal in volume before adding accents.
