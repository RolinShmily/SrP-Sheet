## Playing Notes

This minor pentatonic phrase should feel vocal. Slide into the first target note, keep the bend in tune, and leave a small breath before the final root note.

| Beat | Idea | Detail |

| 2 | Tension | Bend D toward E |
| 3 | Release | Descend to A |
