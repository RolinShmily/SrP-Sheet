## Playing Notes

Use this warmup before practicing songs. Keep the fretting hand close to the strings, let each open chord ring for the full bar, and listen for muted notes before increasing speed.

## Chord Movement

| Bar | Chord | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | G | Relax the wrist |
| 2 | Cadd9 | Keep the third finger planted |
| 3 | Em7 | Let open strings ring |
| 4 | Dsus4 | Count the change early |

## Practice Prompt

Record one pass at 76 bpm and check whether every chord change lands before beat one.
