## Playing Notes

Keep the picked low-string notes short and controlled while the plucked high-string notes ring clearly. The difficulty comes from balancing two different attacks inside one steady subdivision.

## Motion Notes

| Voice | Attack | Dynamic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Low E/A | Pick | Tight and muted |
| B/e | Middle or ring finger | Clear and singing |

## Practice Prompt

Mute the bass line first, then add the upper voice without letting it rush.
